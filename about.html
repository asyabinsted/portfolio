Ever since I was a little girl, I've been fascinated by the way things work and how people interact with them. This curiosity led me to pursue a career in UX/UI design, where I can combine my love for problem-solving with my passion for creating meaningful experiences.

I specialize in designing products, AI experiences, and tools that combine usability, technology, and strategy to simplify complex processes. My approach is always user-centered, data-driven, and focused on creating solutions that not only look beautiful but also solve real problems.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring new technologies, reading about psychology and human behavior, or experimenting with new design tools and methodologies.
